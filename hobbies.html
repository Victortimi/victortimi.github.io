Interests

What I Love Outside Work

📚
Reading & Continuous Learning
A voracious reader across technical papers, popular science books, and African literature. From the latest arXiv preprints on AI to Chinua Achebe — there's always something on the nightstand.
⚽
Football
A passionate football fan who follows the beautiful game with genuine enthusiasm. Whether playing casual 5-a-sides with colleagues in Paris or following the Nigerian Super Eagles — football is a constant.
🌍
Travel & Cultural Exploration
Living in Paris as a Nigerian researcher offers an extraordinary vantage point. Exploring European cities, tasting new cuisines, and understanding diverse cultures feeds a deep curiosity about the human experience.
💻
Coding & Building
When not doing formal research, I enjoy building personal tools, experimenting with new APIs, and tinkering with creative programming projects — from data visualisations to mini LLM experiments.
🎵
Music & Afrobeats
Music is fuel. Afrobeats, Highlife and contemporary jazz are constant companions in the lab. There's something about a good rhythm that makes even the toughest debugging sessions more bearable.
🧘
Fitness & Wellbeing
Maintaining physical and mental health is a serious hobby. Regular gym sessions, long walks along the Seine, and mindful practices help sustain the energy and focus that research demands.
🗣️
Mentoring & Community
Passionate about giving back — mentoring younger students interested in AI and data science, sharing lessons learned, and being part of the growing Nigerian STEM diaspora community in Europe.
✍️
Writing & Knowledge Sharing
Believe strongly that good science must be communicated clearly. Enjoy writing technical summaries, blog-style explainers, and breaking down complex AI concepts for non-specialist audiences.
