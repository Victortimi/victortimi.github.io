PTDF Overseas Scholarship Scheme
The Petroleum Technology Development Fund (PTDF) Overseas Scholarship Scheme is one of Nigeria's most prestigious and competitive academic fellowships, awarded to exceptional individuals to pursue world-class postgraduate education in strategic disciplines. Being awarded this scholarship twice — once for an MSc and once for a PhD — is a rare distinction reflecting consistent academic excellence and professional promise.
