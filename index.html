Active Research
O-RAN
Self-Healing Networks
Explainable AI & Large Language Models for O-RAN Self-Healing
My doctoral research develops XAI-driven frameworks and LLM-powered systems for anomaly detection, root-cause analysis, and knowledge-grounded remediation in Open Radio Access Networks. By making AI reasoning interpretable and actionable, this work aims to enable fully autonomous, self-healing telecommunications infrastructure — a critical capability for next-generation networks.
